Refugees around the world and in conflict areas

KLEINE UITLEG VAN HET PROJECT? My project visualises refugees over the whole world: Where do they come from? and: Where are they going? Besides that, 5 conflict areas are being visualised. Hereby it becomes clear from which country they flee and to which near countries. Also, the gender and age of the refugees are given.
